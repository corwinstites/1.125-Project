Let's get started!

USE THE TOOL

To get started, use the map below to pick the location that you're interested in. Remeber, our model is built from data taken around the US coast, so pick a location near the coast of the continental US, Hawaii, Alaska, or a US territory for best results. Clicking on the map will automatically copy your selected location to your clipboard.











Now that you have your selected latitude and longitude copied to your clipboard, take a look below. We have a Google Colab notebook which executes our SVP prediction model displayed in this window. Notice this window is using a preset example location. To use the tool yourself simply click on the “Open in Colab” button. Once in the notebook simply replace the value of “lat_lon” with the item copied to your clipboard. Select “Runtime” then “Run All.” After the code executes an image of your predicted SVP will automatically be saved to your computer!
